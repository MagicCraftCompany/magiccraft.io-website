Campaign window: 14-20 July 2026, Bangkok time

## Positioning

From 2021 to 2026, MagicCraft grew from a playable fantasy-world project into a
public portfolio that now presents a live game and six focused AI products.
This seven-day series gives each of those seven public products one clear day.

Every day must do three things:

1. Lead with one shipped product or product group.
2. Give people one clear destination.
3. Keep current product stages and limits honest.

## Visual system

- Vertical 4:5 social poster.
- Midnight navy, electric cyan, violet, magenta, and restrained gold.
- Official MagicCraft logo at the top.
- Small `THE BUILD STORY 2021-2026` series line and `DAY X OF 7` badge.
- One large headline, one concise CTA, and `MAGICCRAFT.IO` in the footer.
- Cinematic fantasy-game energy fused with premium AI-product visual language.
- No identifiable real people, third-party logos, token-price charts, or finance imagery.

## Avoid list

- No price, return, ROI, passive-income, market-cap, or investment promises.
- Do not say that all seven products were built five years ago or share one legal owner.
- Do not imply that optional Web3 is required to play the core game.
- Do not call every system fully operational.
- Do not advertise the unavailable Rent testnet.
- Do not present MAGAS7 as beyond early access.
- Keep Merlin connected accounts and messaging permission-based; do not imply every media path is fully verified.
- Label MAGAS7 `EARLY ACCESS` and DragonList `BETA`.
- For DragonList, state that recording requires participant notice and consent.
- Do not imply that DocAI diagnoses, replaces a clinician, or handles emergencies.
- Do not imply that connected accounts, publishing, payments, wallets, or messaging run without user permission.
